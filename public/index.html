Meet the Team

Matt Giulianelli,
DMD, FAGD, FICOI

Doctor Matt Giulianelli received his dental degree at the world-renowned Tufts School of Dental Medicine in Boston, and went on to complete a general practice residency at Boston Medical Center where he received advanced training in many aspects of oral surgery, prosthodontics and endodontics. He earned Fellowship status in the Academy of General Dentistry, an honor only 3% of practicing dentists in the U.S. have achieved. He is also a peer reviewer for research articles submitted to the Academy's journal "General Dentistry".

In his free time, hobbies include learning advanced dentistry, reading, food, exercise, rock climbing, guitar, aviation, and playing around with his children Neesa and Xavier.

Professional Memberships: American Dental Association, Fellow-Academy of General Dentistry, Fellow-International College of Oral Implantologists, American Academy of Cosmetic Dentistry, American Academy of Dental Sleep Medicine, Vermont State Dental Society, and the Lake Champlain Regional Chamber of Commerce

Kadam Giulianelli,
DMD, FICOI

Doctor Kay Giulianelli graduated from New York University before going to Tufts University School of Dental Medicine to earn her dental degree. That is where she met her best friend and husband, Dr. Matt Giulianelli. Dr. Kay successfully completed a general practice residency at Fletcher Allen Health Care where she was exposed to many different aspects of dentistry, including oral surgery and pediatrics. Dr. Kay wants to give her patients healthy and beautiful smiles through care and comfort. She feels it's important to give her patients the best and up-to-date treatment and does so by attending many continuing education courses and reading dental literature.

Dr. Kay enjoys eating all different kinds of foods, traveling, reading, particularly historical fiction, skiing, and watching and discussing movies.

Professional Memberships: Fellow-International College of Oral Implantologists, Academy of General Dentistry, American Academy of Dental Sleep Medicine, and the Lake Champlain Regional Chamber of Commerce.

Heidi Marsano

Heidi is the Guest Service Specialist at Champlain Smile Solutions. She graduated from Essex High in 2001, and has explored careers in both retail management as well as running her own personal training business and working at various health clubs in Chittenden County as a personal trainer.

She strives to offer the best care to all of our guests in a positive and comfortable environment. It is her goal to ensure that every one of our guests leaves our practice feeling that they received the best possible personalized attention in a courteous and professional manner.

Outside of the practice, Heidi enjoys being active, including running in marathons. She also enjoy spending time with her 6 year old son, Ezra.

Jessica Raner

Jessica is the dental assistant at Champlain Smile Solutions. She graduated from the dental assisting program at Center for Technology, Essex over 10 years ago and has worked in both general and surgical dental practices since. She is enthusiastic about her career and really enjoys the opportunity to meet great people like you and your family, and to educate them about oral health.

In her personal time she likes to stay active with her three-year-old son Christian with things like hiking, biking, snowshoeing, and rock climbing.

Jessica Wright

Jessica Wright and is the dental hygienist at Champlain Smile Solutions. She is a graduate of the dental hygiene program at Vermont Technical Center and will continue her education over the next two years and get her Bachelor's Degree in Applied Business Management.

She was born in Virginia but has lived in Vermont for most of her life. She loves to travel, run, and spend time with her family and friends.

She believes that a smile has the power to create a strong impression, and she will work with you to make sure your smile is healthy and one that you love!
